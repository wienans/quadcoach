# Ball and surface naming follows the Quadball rulebook, not Quidditch terms

QuadCoach is a coaching tool for the sport of Quadball (renamed from "Quidditch" in 2022). The three balls and the playing surface use the **generic names adopted by the official Quadball rulebook after the rename** — `Volleyball`, `Dodgeball`, `FlagRunner`, and `court` — rather than the original `Quaffle`, `Bludger`, `Snitch`, and `pitch`.

This is primarily IP/trademark avoidance ( distancing the sport from the Rowling/Warner Bros. "Quidditch" property), and secondarily alignment with the rulebook's current canonical vocabulary. Player positions (`Chaser`, `Beater`, `Keeper`, `Seeker`) are retained because the rulebook kept them.

**Consequence:** do not "fix" these names to Quaffle/Bludger/Snitch. They are intentional. When mapping to the sport for a reader, the canonical mapping is: Volleyball = the scoring ball, Dodgeball = the knockout ball, FlagRunner = the snitch/flag.
